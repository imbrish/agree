We've got what you need!

We are AGREE, a group with a passion for eliminating the headaches of paper contracts. We seek to remove the unnecessary printing and signing of contracts, the stress of not knowing what contracts you are in, and the mess of storing contracts - often unknowingly when you will need them again. No strings attached!

Solve my problems!

Your problems are solved!

Our solution enables businesses and individuals to effortlessly create, sign and store contracts electronically. Your contracts are saved with time-stamp and signatures, enabling perfect overview and indefinite storage horizon.

We bring over 5 years of blockchain experience to enable state-of-the-art solutions for your security. We also partner with BankID to provide the highest standard possible for authentication, guaranteeing enforceability of your contracts.
